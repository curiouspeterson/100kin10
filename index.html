The Story

The Story

100Kin10 is a non-profit with TK partners. In the fall of 2013 they hatched a plan to launch a branding campaign aimed at recruiting more STEM teachers. Partners could all chip in to create something bigger and more amazing than any one partner could accomplish on their own.

Cultivated Wit is a company of creative conspirators who tell stories, build community, make products, and teach. They collide comedy, technology, and design to bring good ideas to “Earth.” They put in a bid to build that branding campaign, and most certainly didn’t blackmail anybody to win it.

Now everybody is married and TK partners have contributed money and flatware to help them begin their life together. This fall, they are expecting to birth a beautiful recruitment campaign. Okay, this metaphor is getting uncomfortable…
